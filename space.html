Space Facts

Here are some facts about space!

General Facts:

  1. Space is completely silent.
  2. There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on all the beaches on Earth.
  3. Space is completely dark.
  4. Space has microgravity

Facts about the planets:

  • Mercury is the smallest planet
  • Venus is the hottest planet
  • Earth is the only planet known to support life
  • Mars used to be like Earth
  • Jupiter has a storm called the Great Red Spot
  • Saturn is known as the Lord of the Rings
  • Uranus is the only planet that rotates on its side
  • Neptune is the windiest planet

Facts about the sun:

  • The sun is the only star in our solar system
  • The sun is 4.6 billion years old
  • The sun is 93 million miles away from Earth
